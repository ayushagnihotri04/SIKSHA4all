    Frequently Asked Questions

    What study materials are available on this website?

    We provide a variety of study materials, including handwritten notes, eBooks, previous year question papers (PYQs), video lectures, and textbooks in PDF format.

    How can I download study materials?

    You can browse the available resources and download them by clicking the Download button next to each material. Some resources may require you to sign in.

    Are the study materials free?

    Yes! Most of our resources are free to access. However, some premium content may require a subscription.

    How can I access video lectures?

    You can watch video lectures directly on our website. Just navigate to the Videos section and select the subject or topic you want to learn.

    Can I request specific study materials?

    Yes! If you need specific study materials that are not available, you can request them through the Request Study Material section, and we’ll try to provide them.

    How can teachers contribute to the platform?

    Teachers can upload study materials, create assignments, and manage students by signing up as a teacher. You can share lesson plans, teaching guides, and reference materials.

    How do I manage my students?

    Once registered as a teacher, you can assign tasks, mark attendance, and track students’ progress through your dashboard.

    Are there any teaching resources available?

    Yes! We provide lesson plans, teaching guides, reference eBooks, and recorded lectures to help teachers enhance their teaching methods.

    How do I sign up on the website?

    Click on the Sign Up button and choose whether you’re a student or teacher. Fill in the required details, and you’ll be able to access the resources immediately.

    Whom do I contact for support?

    If you have any issues, you can reach out to our support team via email at support@yourwebsite.com or use the Contact Us page.
